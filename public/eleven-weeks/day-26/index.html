Shane got 3.5 hours of therapy today.

He transferred to the chair. Sam and I played a card game with him. We played War and he took every trick but the last. We tied and then I had the last high card. üíó

He also went to the PT room and then went to a hallway where they practiced standing and sitting and taking steps. He did great. And insisted that he lift himself by pushing off the armrests of the chair.

He also asked them a ton of questions about how long they would work with him. And if they had done it before.

He slept very soundly in between sessions. And when he woke up, he was more alert than I’d seen yet.

They removed the trach. He seems so relieved. It’s bandaged and we can hear his voice clearly.

He got upgraded to normal food. Dinner was the first meal. Fish, mashed potatoes and gravy, beets, apple juice, milk, yogurt and fruit juice. He said it tasted good.

At that moment Gary and Christia Blackburn were there. And he asked if we had come just to watch him eat. üòÖ

He also had dozens of questions, including how long things were going to last. Also about what Gary and Christia had done for work that day. He asked about getting a cabin. We believe that means a Wallkill cabin. Because Gary had told him about a possible camping trip in the fall. He asked question after question and laughed really hard a few times. Including when Gary put the helmet on.

He asked why he was there. We got to explain what happened. He said he didn’t remember any of that.

After we mentioned him falling outside the office, then he asked for Jeremy by name about four times on different occasions. ‚ò∫Ô∏è
